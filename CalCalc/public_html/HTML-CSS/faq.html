CalCalc - your personal health calculator

What is CalCalc?

CalCalc is a mobile application providing accessibility to nutrition values .

How do I get nutrition values?

Simply press MENU, "home" and write your food (tomato for example) in the text box.

Why do I need to sign in for?

First you don't have to sign in in order to receive a simple nutrition value.

Sign in allow us to deliver you advanced features such as recommended meals according to your previous searches.
